About This Setting

The Vegas Domain

Las Vegas represents one of the most unique Camarilla domains in North America. Built on excess, greed, and the constant flow of mortal tourists, it provides an endless feeding ground for the Kindred who have claimed it. The city's 24/7 nature, transient population, and culture of hedonism make it the perfect hunting ground—and the perfect place for ancient grudges to play out under neon lights.

Power Structures & Politics

The Kindred of Las Vegas don't just feed on tourists—they own the infrastructure that brings them here. From the massive casino complexes to the entertainment venues, vampire elders pull strings that determine the fate of millions of visitors. But beneath the glittering facade, the same ancient conflicts that have plagued Kindred society for millennia continue to simmer.

  • The Camarilla's Golden Grip: Traditional vampires who've turned the Strip into their personal empire
  • Anarch Undercurrents: Younger Kindred challenging the old ways in downtown Vegas and the tunnels below
  • The Thin-Blood Underground: New vampires struggling to find their place in an established hierarchy
  • Second Inquisition Eyes: Government agencies using Vegas as a testing ground for anti-vampire operations
